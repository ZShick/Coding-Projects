Event Space

Located on Talos, World's Edge has a long history as a place of value for Miners.

The beautiful yet dangerous terrain has been long abandoned by corporations but is the perfect environment for our form of entertainment.

We have kept the train operable which allows wonderful views for out guests to take in the action.

We have lava rivers, frozen cities, abandoned factories, and even explosive geysers! Come watch the Apex Games here. The baddest contest in the Outlands.
